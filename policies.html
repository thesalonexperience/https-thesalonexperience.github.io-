Salon Policies

This is a private luxury salon experience created to provide intentional care, peace, and professionalism. By booking, you agree to the following policies.

Deposits

A $25 non-refundable deposit is required for all salon services.

House call services require a 50% deposit of the total service cost. Deposits secure your appointment time and are applied toward your service total.

Same-Day Appointments

Same-day appointments are subject to availability and include a $30 same-day convenience fee.

Late Policy

A 5-minute grace period is provided.

After the grace period, a $5 fee is applied every 5 minutes. Appointments exceeding 15 minutes late may be canceled to protect the salon flow.

Private Salon Policy

This is a private salon environment.

Only the client receiving services is permitted inside the salon. No additional guests are allowed unless pre-approved.

Skin Pampering Disclaimer

No licensed esthetician services are provided. No medical, corrective, or therapeutic treatments are offered.

Lash Services Disclaimer

Lash services are performed by a technician with licensure obtained by the State of Michigan.

Open Availability
