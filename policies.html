Salon Policies

Luxury Private Salon Experience

This is a private luxury salon suite designed to provide a calm, focused, and elevated experience. Only the client receiving service is permitted inside the salon. Additional guests, companions, or observers are not allowed.

Deposits & Payments

A deposit is required to secure all appointments.

Salon Appointments: $25 non-refundable deposit.
House Call Appointments: 50% of the total service cost is required at booking.

Deposits are applied toward the total service balance. Remaining balances are due at the time of service.

House Call Experience

House call services are offered as a premium convenience experience. A house call fee applies and is based on travel, time, and service requirements. Exact pricing will be displayed during booking.

Same-Day Appointments

Same-day appointments are available for an additional $30 convenience fee and are subject to availability.

Late Policy

A 5-minute grace period is provided. After the grace period, a $5 late fee is applied for every additional 5 minutes. Excessive lateness may result in appointment cancellation with deposit forfeited.

Children’s Appointments & Cooperation

Children must be able to sit calmly and cooperate for the duration of the service. If a child is unable to cooperate or significantly disrupts the service, a Behavior Management Fee may be applied.

This fee is assessed at the stylist’s discretion to account for additional time, effort, and service interruption.

Privacy & Consent

By booking an appointment, clients acknowledge and agree to all salon policies. A privacy acknowledgment and consent confirmation is required at checkout.

Availability Notice

Appointments are currently open to the public. Subscription-based booking may be required in the future.
