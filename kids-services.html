Kids Hair Menu (Ages 5–15)

The Kids Hair Experience is offered in a calm, private luxury salon environment designed to support comfort, patience, and intentional care.

This experience is best suited for children who are able to sit calmly and cooperate throughout the duration of the service.

Important Salon Policy

This is a private luxury salon experience. Only the child receiving service may be present unless otherwise discussed.

If a child is unable to cooperate or repeatedly interrupts the service, a Behavior Management Fee may be applied. This fee reflects the additional time, patience, and focus required to complete the service with care.
